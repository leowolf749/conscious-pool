Leo Wolf is a musician and artist from Charlotte, North Carolina. Using various synthesis, sampling, looping techniques, and field recordings he creates warm textural soundscapes, often falling under the experimental/ambient/drone genres. His weapon of choice for recording and live performance is a eurorack modular synthesizer.

Leo takes inspiration from symbolism, and both conceptual and natural phenomena including dreams, reflections, memory, and time. The labyrinth is a recurring symbol and source of inspiration as it represents the cyclical nature of existence and the parallels between the physical and metaphysical world. "Portal hunting" is a phrase Leo uses to describe seeking out these connections between outer and inner, as a way of seeing or feeling beyond the veil of our perception and understanding.

In 2019 Leo began performing and collaborating with Sinetology, a Charlotte-based electronic music label and collective. Sinetology features a multi-genre catalog of artists and releases ranging from techno/acid/house to ambient/noise and blurring the lines in between.
