Leo Wolf is a musician and artist from Charlotte, North Carolina. Using various synthesis, sampling, and looping techniques he creates warm textural soundscapes, often falling under the experimental/ambient/drone genres. His weapon of choice for recording and live performance is a eurorack modular synthesizer.

Leo takes inspiration from symbolism, and conceptual and natural phenomena including dreams, reflections, memory, and time. The labyrinth is a recurring symbol and source of inspiration as it represents the cyclical nature of existence and the parallels between the physical and metaphysical world. Actively seeking out these symbolic phenomena can be described as "Portal Hunting", a phrase coined by Leo.

In 2019 Leo began performing and collaborating with Sinetology, a Charlotte-based electronic music label and collective. Sinetology features a multi-genre catalog of artists and releases ranging from techno/acid/house to ambient/noise and blurring the lines in between.
